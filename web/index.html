What folder should I pick?
• Just a single .jsonl — basic attribution.
• A project folder under projects/ — all sessions for that project; pick one from the list.
• Your whole ~/.claude folder — every session, plus CLAUDE.md and skill attribution.
Browsers can't auto-navigate to a specific path. Chrome/Edge remember your last pick across visits.
